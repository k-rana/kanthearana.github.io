Don’t you care about what we had? But now I can see why. There’s a fundamental part of my personality back then that was too selfish to see that I was also doing things wrong, and now that I realize it, There’s nothing I can do but learn. Use this information to do right by my new relationships and treat others right. I know that you and I are done now, but I just wanted to say thank you, because you were my best friend during my formative years and you are probably the reason I am who I am today. I’m not sure how I’d turn out if you hadn’t been there with me, but I know I would be very different from myself now. Losing you hurt like hell, but i know that because I knew you, I have been changed for good.
